Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Proof store my photos?

Proof never moves your photos. It references them in place, the same way Lightroom does. Your originals stay exactly where they are on disk.

Where is my catalog stored?

Your catalog is stored in a SQLite database at ~/Library/Application Support/Proof/. Back it up like any other important file.

How do I add a folder?

Click the + button in the sidebar and choose a folder. Proof indexes it immediately and builds thumbnails in the background.

Does Proof support Fuji RAW files?

Yes. Proof supports RAW files from all major camera brands, as well as HEIF/HEIC files.

How do I delete rejected photos?

Mark shots as Rejected with the X key, then press Backspace / Delete. Proof moves rejected photos to the macOS Trash.

I removed a folder but my photos are still on disk - is that normal?

Yes. "Remove from catalog" only removes the catalog entry so Proof stops tracking that folder. Files on disk are never touched.

Does Proof work without an internet connection?

Yes. Proof is entirely local. No internet connection is required for any feature. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics.

Is Proof a Lightroom replacement?

Proof covers the culling and library management side of a Lightroom workflow - flagging, rating, comparing, and organizing. If you need deep non-destructive editing, a dedicated editor pairs well with Proof.

How do I report a bug?

Email hello@lunero.com.au with your macOS version and a description of what happened. Screenshots or screen recordings are helpful.
