Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Proof does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Your Photos

Proof accesses photos on your Mac solely to display thumbnails and allow you to view, rate, and cull them. No photos, metadata, or file paths are uploaded to any server. Everything stays on your device.

Your Catalog

All ratings, flags, adjustments, and settings are stored in a SQLite database on your Mac at ~/Library/Application Support/Proof/. This file never leaves your device.

Analytics

Proof does not use any analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting services. No usage data is collected.

Third-Party Services

Proof does not include analytics SDKs, advertising networks, cloud sync, or telemetry services. On launch, Proof may contact GitHub to check whether a newer version is available. That update check does not upload your photos, catalog, edits, metadata, or file paths.

Security

Proof is signed and notarized for macOS. Read the Security & Trust page for verification commands, update-channel details, and local cache locations.

Questions

Use the support form if you have a privacy question or want to request a review of your data handling concern.
